Redwood Creek

Location

Elevation: 31’

Begins near Canada College, crosses I-280 at the Farm Hill Road junction, heads south and then turns southeast. Turns towards the northeast and crosses I-280 again at the SR-84 junction. Continues northeast, becomes a concrete channel at Alameda De Las Pulgas (former USGS stream gage). Confluence with Arroyo Ojo de Agua and then empties into the Bay.

NOAA Atlas 14 Precipitation Frequency Estimates:

Hydrology

Creek needs prolonged high-intensity rainfall in order to flood, possibly a 1/50 or 1/100 year event in the range of 60 min – 3 days. Saturated soils also lower precipitation requirement (7 – 30 day totals).
